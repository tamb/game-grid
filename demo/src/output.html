GameGrid Demos

 

Coin Collector

Use WASD or Arrow Keys (or the on-screen pad on smaller screens) to travel around the maze and collect coins.

You can wrap on both the X and Y axis

Note: This map is randomly generated. If you are unable to complete the maze please click the "Regenerate Maze" button
